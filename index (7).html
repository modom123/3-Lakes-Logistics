Our Story

Built by Drivers.
Run for Drivers.

Founded on one belief: owner-operators deserve a dispatching partner that truly goes to work for them — a real team that negotiates hard and answers every call.

Who We Are

Freight Logistics &
Dispatching

3 Lakes Logistics is a fully licensed freight logistics and dispatching company co-founded by Mark Odom and Deandre Richard. We operate as both a dispatching service for independent owner-operators and a growing asset-based carrier — giving us insight from both sides of the windshield.

Our dual-model approach means dispatching fees provide consistent income while our own fleet generates higher-margin freight revenue. Both fuel each other's growth — and that benefits every carrier partner we serve.

We are FMCSA licensed, fully bonded, and covered by all required commercial insurance. Every carrier verified, every broker vetted, every load tracked to delivery.
