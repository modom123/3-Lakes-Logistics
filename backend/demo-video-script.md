# 3 Lakes Logistics — 3-Minute Demo Video Script

**Target audience:** Owner-operators and small fleet operators (~50-100 trucks)
**Length:** 2:45–3:00 minutes
**Tone:** Confident, blue-collar, solution-focused (not corporate)

---

## 00:00–00:15 — Hook (The Problem)

**Visual:** Show a dispatcher at a desk looking stressed, manual dispatch system showing on screen.

> "You're running your own authority. You've got 5–50 trucks. And every week, you're manually matching loads with drivers. Phone calls, emails, spreadsheets. It's costing you money and burning you out."

---

## 00:15–00:35 — The Solution

**Visual:** Switch to phone/app screen showing automated dispatch. Drivers receiving loads automatically.

> "What if your dispatch ran itself? No more manual work. No more phone calls. Just loads flowing to your drivers automatically, in real-time."
> 
> "That's the 3 Lakes Founders program. We automate your dispatch. You keep 100% of your earnings. And it costs just $300 a month. Lifetime lock."

---

## 00:35–01:05 — Why It Works (The System)

**Visual:** Show the execution engine workflow. Leads → Matching → Dispatch → Tracking → Payouts.

> "Here's what we do:
> - **Find loads** — DAT, Truckstop, Echo Global, Uber Freight. We pull from everywhere.
> - **Match them automatically** — To YOUR trucks. Your lanes. Your rates.
> - **Dispatch to drivers** — Via app. One tap. No confusion.
> - **Track in real-time** — Where your loads are, where your trucks are, what you're making.
> - **Auto-payout** — Drivers get paid Friday. You get paid Friday. No spreadsheets."

---

## 01:05–01:45 — The Real Wins

**Visual:** Dashboard showing: revenue per truck, hours saved per week, driver retention metrics.

> "The wins that matter:
>
> - **$2-3K more per truck per month** — Because automation finds the best loads, not just what calls in.
> - **20+ hours back per week** — No more dispatch grind.
> - **Better drivers** — They stay longer because the work is automatic and reliable.
> - **Predictable cash flow** — You know what you're making every Friday."

---

## 01:45–02:30 — The Commitment (Founders = Locked-In Price)

**Visual:** Show the pricing chart. $300/mo, highlight "Founders = Locked In Forever."

> "The Founders program is simple: $300 a month. Forever. That's our commitment to early adopters who trust us to grow with them.
>
> No per-load fees. No percentage of earnings. No surprise price hikes in 18 months.
>
> $300. All your loads automated. 100% of your earnings."

---

## 02:30–02:55 — Social Proof + CTA

**Visual:** Show 2-3 testimonial clips or quick stats. (Optional: Driver testimonials, truck stats)

> "Founders are already running with us. Dispatch automation is working. Revenue is up.
>
> If you've got 5+ trucks, and you're tired of manual dispatch, let's talk. A quick 15-minute call to see if this works for YOUR situation."

**CTA on screen:** "Book Your Demo Call → [Calendly URL]"

---

## 02:55–03:00 — Sign-Off

> "I'm the Commander. Let's get you from manual to automatic.
> 
> See you on the call."

---
