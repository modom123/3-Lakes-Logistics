# 3 Lakes Logistics — Carrier Onboarding Guide

**Welcome to the Founders Program**

You've been selected to join our Founders tier — a lifetime commitment to owner-operators and small fleets who want to scale without the headaches.

## The 5-Step Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Founders Call with Nova
**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**What happens:** Nova (our AI prospecting agent) calls you to understand your operation.

We talk about:
- How long you've been running your own authority
- Your current fleet size and equipment
- Where you typically run lanes
- Your biggest pain point right now (manual dispatch? fuel costs? load sourcing?)

**What you need:** Just a phone call. Have your DOT# handy if you know it.

**When:** Within 24 hours of signing up

---

### Step 2: Email Follow-Up — How We Work
**What happens:** Nova sends you an email explaining the Founders process so you know exactly what's coming.

This covers the 5 steps and answers common questions like:
- "How long does onboarding take?" (Usually 3–5 business days)
- "What do I need to provide?" (See Step 3)
- "When can I start getting loads?" (Day 1 after signing)

**Action:** Read it. You don't need to do anything yet.

---

### Step 3: Commander Call
**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**What happens:** You hop on a brief call with our human Commander.

This is where we dig deeper into YOUR situation. We talk about:
- Your preferred lanes and equipment types
- Target RPM (revenue per mile) and home time needs
- Any equipment you're looking to add
- How the Founders program works (pricing, process, support)

**What you need:** Just be ready to chat. Have any questions ready.

**Scheduling:** Book via the link in Nova's email (Calendly)

---

### Step 4: Onboarding Packet — Sign Everything
**What happens:** You receive ONE email from Adobe Sign with your complete onboarding packet.

**What's in the packet:**

| Document | What to do |
|---|---|
| **Dispatch Agreement** | Read, review, e-sign. This is your contract with us. Covers rates ($300/mo Founders lock), load terms, dispute resolution, etc. Takes ~3 min. |
| **W9 Form** | IRS tax form (blank). Fill in your name, business name, EIN/SSN, address, entity type. E-sign it. Takes ~3 min. |
| **Certificate of Insurance** | You don't sign this — just upload it from your insurance company. We verify coverage before you can accept loads. |

**Timeline:** Everything takes about 10 minutes total.

**What we do:** 
- Verify your insurance is current (covers cargo and liability)
- Run your FMCSA safety check (CSA scores, out-of-service history)
- Set up your banking for direct deposit of earnings

---

### Step 5: Go Live
**What happens:** We flip the switch and you're active.

**Day 1:**
- Your carrier account is live
- You get access to the 3 Lakes Logistics app (iOS/Android)
- Loads start flowing to your app in real-time
- Your first check-call happens (we confirm you've got your load and understand the delivery point)

**You get:**
- **100% of load earnings** — no commission, no hidden fees
- **Lifetime $300/month rate** — locked in as a Founder
- **Full automation** — loads sent to your app, rate confirmations sent electronically, check-calls every 2–4 hours
- **Human support** — our Commander is one call away for any issue
- **Weekly metrics** — RPM reports, lane optimization recommendations, capacity planning

## FAQ

**Q: Can I work with other brokers while in the Founders program?**  
A: Yes. You own your authority. You can take loads from anyone. We just ask that 3 Lakes is your primary source (makes economic sense for you too).

**Q: What if I need to stop for a month?**  
A: No problem. Your $300/month hold your pricing. You can pause anytime, come back anytime.

**Q: How do I get paid?**  
A: Direct deposit every day. Your loads close → invoice is paid → money in your account within 24 hours usually.

**Q: What if I have an accident or safety issue?**  
A: We have a compliance team that monitors CSA scores and safety records monthly. If something comes up, we work with you to fix it (most issues are fixable). Your Founders pricing is secure as long as you stay legal and safe.

**Q: How many loads per week should I expect?**  
A: Depends on your lanes and availability. New Founders typically see 2–4 loads per week in their preferred lanes. High-utilization carriers see 4–6+.

**Q: Can I customize my availability (lanes, equipment, home time)?**  
A: Yes. Your profile is fully customizable. Prefer the Northeast? No hazmat? Need to be home every 5 days? We build your profile around your needs.
